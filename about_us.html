About us..

We’re not like other booking platforms. We give your business a fully personalised experience, allowing you to reach out to your customers with ease. We stay open to the changing winds of business, helping you to adapt to your growth.

Who we are

As two best friends in our 30’s who like to take care of our appearance, we found that booking processes on other applications were a bit of a hassle. The idea for That Time came about when we eventually had enough. We decided to create the platform that we so desperately needed.

Through a love of barbering,

sales and operations experience behind us, we set out to define what our core principles would be when creating our application. Fundamentally, we didn’t want anyone to have a bad booking experience ever again. We also wanted to allow the businesses we work with to put their customers at the heart of what they do.

Small businesses thrive on their dedicated and involved customer base, and we wanted them to have access to a platform that could help them to maximise their profits in a simple and effective way.

Our Values are Simple

We offer support to help grow your business through innovation, creating memorable moments between you and your customers. With strong partnership we will elevate customer loyalty by delivering excellence.

Our vision

More Opportunities

Through That Time, we want to give our vendors more opportunities to build a community with their customers, bringing them closer together to form long-lasting bonds with brand loyalty. We want to eliminate the headaches of day-to-day admin issues by providing a simple and intuitive platform to assist in everyday booking tasks.

We want to ensure that our platform gives clear and accessible data insights for vendors, allowing them to capitalise and increase profits. We want to simplify the booking process, allowing customers to get appointments when they really need them, which allows businesses to thrive and maximise their work hours. Creating transparency between the vendor and the customer is what we are all about.
